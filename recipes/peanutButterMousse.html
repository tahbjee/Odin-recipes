Peanut butter mousse

Photo by Yolanda Gutierrez

Peanut butter mousse is a luscious and creamy dessert made with the delightful combination of whipped cream, peanut butter, and sweeteners.

The dominant taste of peanut butter is complemented by a hint of sweetness, often from added sugar, and sometimes enriched with a touch of vanilla extract for extra depth.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream

Instructions

  1. Whip the Heavy Cream: In a large bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. This can be done with a hand mixer or stand mixer.
  2. Mix Peanut Butter and Sugar: In a separate bowl, mix the smooth peanut butter and powdered sugar until well combined.
  3. Combine Peanut Butter Mixture with Whipped Cream: Gently fold the peanut butter mixture into the whipped cream until smooth and well incorporated. Be careful not to deflate the whipped cream. depending on the size of the meatballs.
  4. Add Vanilla Extract: Stir in the vanilla extract, ensuring it is evenly distributed throughout the mixture.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate the mousse for at least 2 hours to allow it to set and develop its flavor. You can also leave it in the fridge overnight for a firmer texture.
